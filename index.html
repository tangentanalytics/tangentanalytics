Who We Are

About Tangent Analytics

Tangent Analytics is a specialized consulting firm based in Ottawa, Canada, built on a foundation of deep expertise in geospatial science, data analytics, and business intelligence.

We bring world-class capabilities in spatial analysis, data visualization, and BI development to organizations of all sizes. Our approach is grounded in the kind of rigorous methodology used in international development, public health surveillance, and large-scale population programs — applied to solve your unique challenges.

Whether you need to map complex spatial patterns, build interactive dashboards, automate data pipelines, or upskill your team, Tangent Analytics combines technical depth with practical, results-oriented solutions that deliver measurable impact.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with data-driven insights and geospatial intelligence that enable informed decision-making, operational efficiency, and lasting impact.

Our Vision

To be a trusted partner for organizations seeking to unlock the full potential of their data — bridging the gap between raw information and strategic action through innovative analytics and capacity building.

Our Values

Precision, transparency, and impact. We believe that every dataset tells a story, and our job is to help you read it clearly, act on it confidently, and share it effectively.
